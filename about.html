About Me.

I am a fourth-year mathematical physics undergraduate student at the University of Waterloo, minoring in pure mathematics and astrophysics. After graduating, I hope to attend graduate school in physics, with the goal of working in research.

I am particularly interested in early-universe cosmology, computational astronomy, and particle physics.

I enjoy taking part in science outreach programs, especially ones aiming to promote interest in science to primary and secondary school students. I have been involved in the University of Waterloo's Science Outreach program, as well as in Let's Talk Science.
